# Game Controls

- **Arrow Keys**: Move your character  
- **Y**: Sword attack  
- **X**: Shield block  
- **C**: Interact (open chest, pick up item, talk, open door, etc.)  
- **Enter**: Skip dialogue or text  
- **M**: Open map (if found)

---

# Cheat Keys

- **G**: Toggle invincibility ON  
- **F**: Toggle invincibility OFF  
- **I**: Get all items  
- **H**: Fully heal  
- **J**: Reduce to one health
